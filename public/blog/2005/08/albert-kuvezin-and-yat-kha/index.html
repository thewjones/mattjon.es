Albert Kuvezin and Yat-Kha

According to the Guardian, Albert Kuvezin has such a deep voice that “He makes Barry White sound like Jimmy Somerville â€¦”. It’s true, and you can hear his remarkable throat singing techique on Monday’s Late Junction (until 8/8/2005). Update: You may want to stay tuned to Monday’s Late Junction for a cracking version of Led Zep’s Whole Lotta Love by The Cato Salsa Experience and The Thing.
