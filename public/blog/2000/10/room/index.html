r|o|o|m|

r|o|o|m| was a project I did last year for ‘Visual Arts North East’. I got people to manipulate individual images of a Quicktime VR and email them back to me. I then placed the returned images into the VR and projected it into the same room for the exhibition. It’s the only piece of ‘art’ i’ve done that I am satisfied with and I got loads of positive feedback from it. I’m trying to think of ideas for a new project, so if anyone out there would like to collaborate on something, let me know.
