Film & Music

There’s so many good films and albums being released at the moment. Go and see Amelie if you haven’t do so already. Other films to look forward to are The Coen Brother’s latest, The Man Who Wasn’t There [view trailer] and the film that sees David Lynch’s return to weirdness Mulholland Drive. An album I’m particularly looking forward to is a new release by Hope Sandoval [of Mazzy Star] entitled Bavarian Fruit Bread.
