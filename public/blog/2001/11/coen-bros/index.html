Coen Bro's

Film Unlimited reports that Catherine Zeta-Jones will star alongside George Clooney in The Coen Brother’s film Intolerable Cruelty which will be the next film after To The White Sea. Let’s hope ‘Intolerable Cruelty’ is some kind of working title because it sounds like one of those awful ‘made for TV’ films they show on Channel 5. I went to see The Man Who Wasn’t There a few weeks ago and really enjoyed it; neo-noir dead-pan comedy at its best.
