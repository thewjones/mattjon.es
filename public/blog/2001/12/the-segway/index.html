The Segway

The unveiling of Dean Kamen’s revolutionary transport device is imminent. This page will post video footage of the event at some point tomorrow. A link was posted at forum on the web at the moment. Much of the discussion is based around this alleged report from the Associated Press which describes the device in greater detail. I have to say that the more I read it, the more it sounds like the next Sinclair C5. I guess we’ll have to wait until tomorrow [Monday] for the truth behind all the hype.
