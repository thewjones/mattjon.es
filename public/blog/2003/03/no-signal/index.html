No Signal

After close analysis of 150 results from the SETI@Home project, scientists have found nothing to suggest that they contain evidence of extra-terrestrial life. BBC News has the story, although I wish more thought had been put into the article’s rather cliched headline; “ET Fails to ‘phone home’” suggests that extra-terrestrials originated on Earth, which is not only ridiculous but a complete contradiction in terms.
